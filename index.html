Common Questions

Everything you need to know about enrolling and training at ACA Cricket Academy.

Still Have Questions?
What age groups does ACA accept?
+
ACA accepts players across multiple age groups — from Under-14 through to adult aspirants. Our programs are structured to suit different stages of cricketing development, from juniors to senior-level hopefuls.
How does the selection process work?
+
Candidates go through a rigorous selection trial conducted by ACA coaching staff. Over 3,000 players have been screened to date, with only 40 elite places awarded in the flagship programs. Apply through our registration form and our admissions team will contact you with trial details.
Is ACA a BCCI-affiliated academy?
+
ACA Cricket Academy is a unit of the Assam Cricket Association (ACA). While we are not directly BCCI-affiliated as an academy, our parent body — the Assam Cricket Association — operates under the BCCI framework. Our programs are designed to prepare players for Assam and national-level competition.
Where is the academy located?
+
Our permanent campus is situated at Fulung, North Guwahati, Assam. The campus was inaugurated in August 2025 and features world-class facilities including cricket pitches, professional nets, a fully-equipped gym, and on-site accommodation for residential students.
What programs are available?
+
We offer four structured programs: (1) Residential Training Program — full-time immersive coaching; (2) High-Performance Advance Coaching — elite-level training for state-level aspirants; (3) Personal & Individual Programs — one-on-one tailored sessions; and (4) Pay & Play — flexible access to training facilities.
How do I apply or register?
+
Simply fill out the registration form on this page or email us at info@acacricketacademy.com. Our admissions team will respond within 48 hours with all the information you need to begin your ACA journey.
